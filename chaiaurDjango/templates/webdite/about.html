About Chai aur Code

Brewing ideas over a hot cup — we believe every great project starts with the perfect chai.

Our Story

"Chai aur Code" started with a group of developers who needed more than just caffeine — they needed culture, warmth, and a little spice. We began our journey by crafting premium chai blends that stimulate creativity, comfort the soul, and fuel long coding nights.

Whether you're a designer, student, writer, or full-stack wizard — our chai is brewed to keep you going. One sip, and you're in the flow.

Our Mission

To bring authentic, handcrafted chai to the digital workspace — because innovation tastes better with tradition. We strive to connect the vibrant chai culture with modern creators through taste, technology, and thoughtful storytelling.
